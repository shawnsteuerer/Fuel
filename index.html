Common questions

Everything you
wanted to ask.

Is this actually free? +
Yes — the calorie calculator, macro targets, and sample meal plans are completely free. No credit card, no account needed. The only paid feature is unlimited AI meal generation, which is $7.99/month.
How is this different from MyFitnessPal? +
MyFitnessPal is a food diary — you log everything you eat after the fact. Fuel tells you what to eat before you eat it. No barcode scanning, no obsessive tracking, no guilt. Just a simple plan to follow.
Do I have to log my food every day? +
Never. That's the whole point. Fuel gives you a plan — you follow it. No logging, no weighing chicken, no tracking every bite. Most people quit food logging within 2 weeks. Fuel is built so you don't have to.
How accurate is the calorie calculator? +
We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the gold standard used by registered dietitians. It's accurate within about 5% for most people. We recommend recalculating every 4–6 weeks as your weight changes.
Is it safe? Will I lose muscle? +
Yes, it's safe. When you're in a calorie deficit, Fuel automatically raises your protein target to 35%+ of calories specifically to protect lean muscle. This is standard practice in sports nutrition. If you have a medical condition, always check with your doctor first.
What if I'm vegetarian, vegan, or have dietary restrictions? +
Fuel supports 10 dietary preferences including vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian, keto, paleo, gluten-free, dairy-free, halal, and kosher. The AI meal generator tailors every meal day to your preference.
How does the AI meal generator work? +
It uses Claude AI to generate a completely personalized day of eating — breakfast, snacks, lunch, and dinner — built around your exact calorie and macro targets. Every generation is unique. Free users get one try, Pro users get unlimited.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. Cancel from your account settings at any time and you won't be charged again. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.
